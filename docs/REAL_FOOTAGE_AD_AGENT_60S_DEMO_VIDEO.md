# Aivora Real-Footage Ads 60s Demo Video

## Video objective

Create a polished 60-second SaaS-style walkthrough video for the `/demo/real-footage-ads` landing page hero. The video should help non-technical customers and early investors understand what Aivora Real-Footage Ads does, why it matters, and how the workflow turns messy real footage into a repeatable creative testing loop.

The video is a product walkthrough and positioning asset. It should not imply mature automation beyond the current MVP, and it should not claim proven performance lift.

## Target audience

- Small business owners with real product, store, food, service, or UGC footage.
- DTC, local service, and creator-commerce teams that need more short-form ad variants.
- Agencies that manage many low-budget creative tests for clients.
- Early investors evaluating whether Aivora has a clear wedge beyond generic AI video generation.

## Core message

Aivora turns messy real product footage into multiple testable short-form ad concepts, renders a 9:16 video, and learns from performance data to generate the next round.

Supporting message:

- Most customers already have raw footage.
- The bottleneck is turning that footage into structured, testable ad variants.
- Aivora creates a repeatable loop: upload, index, generate concepts, review, render, import metrics, improve the next round.
